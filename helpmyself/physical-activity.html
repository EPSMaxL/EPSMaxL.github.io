Working out

Recently, I have been getting back into working out, especially arms as I enjoy that the most.

Four months ago, in early June 2021, I got my own cage with olympic weights and bar at home, which made it much easier for me to workout whenever I wanted.

I started out pretty weak compared to how strong I was before COVID and could go to the gym.


Video of me the day of getting the home gym.

Since then, I've worked hard to increase how much I can bench press and can now do the weight in the video for 15 reps instead of one.

I look forward to working out often and feel accomplished after working hard. Due to it being at home, it is pretty easy for me to take a quick break from whatever i'm working on to lift.

I'm proud of how much work I've put in and the results I've seen. It has greatly helped my mental health to work towards a physical goal of increasing my personal records.
